On-chain signaling will monitor mina mainnet or testnet transaction messages.

Scraping App will check the memo message of a transaction and store it in Neo4j DB.

Data stored in DB will be fetched and displayed in ReactJS app with WalletID(s).

Standard format to vote for hard fork needs to be mentioned in ReactJS app.

In summary there will be two apps in this repo, one is to scrape the on-chain data and another one is to fetch from DB.
